Clear the Prove You Can Apply to-do

  • For the question ”Is a HRT interview required”, select ‘No’.
  • Click ‘Done’.

Create a claimant to-do

  • Select template ‘Update information about your nationality’
  • Copy the following into the ‘Why does the claimant need to update their nationality information?’ box:

    ‘Claimant nationality is British Citizen and has not bene out of the country for more than 4 weeks in the last 2 years’

  • Set the deadline date to the end of the first two assessment periods
  • Select 'create to-do'

Advise the person claiming, to sign into their account and clear this to-do:

  • For the question 'Are you a British Citizen?', tell them to select 'Yes'
  • For the question 'In the last 2 years, have you been out of the UK for more than 4 weeks at a time?', tell them to select 'No'
  • Remind the person claiming to check their UC account for updates on their claim
