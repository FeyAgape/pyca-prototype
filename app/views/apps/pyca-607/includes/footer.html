How to clear the Prove You Can Apply to-do

  1. For the question 'Is a HRT interview required?' select 'No'.
  2. Click ‘Done’.

How to clear the Refer to Decision Maker to-do

  1. Download the ALP.
  2. Complete the appropriate section in the ALP using the necessary evidence.
  3. Upload the ALP.
  4. For the question ‘Is this a fast-track HRT referral case?’, select ‘Yes’.
  5. Click ‘Done’.
