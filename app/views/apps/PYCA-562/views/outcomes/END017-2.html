How to clear the 'Prove You Can Apply' to-do:

  1. In the ‘PYCA to do’ select ‘No’ for "Is an HRT interview needed?”
  2. Clear the to do

How to book a further evidence interview:

  1. Download the Decision Makers' report below
  2. Upload this report to the agent notes in the’Refer to Decision Maker' to-do
  3. Upload all the claimant’s evidence to the agent notes in the 'Refer to Decision Maker' to-do
  4. In the claimant's history add "PYCA completed, 'Refer to DM' to do to be cleared once the evidence is provided"
  5. Do not clear the 'Refer to Decision Maker' to-do
  6. Book a further evidence interview
  7. Add this note to ‘Claimant’s journal’: “Attach the evidence provided by the claimant to the 'Refer to Decision Maker to do' and clear the to-do”.
