You may be able to apply

Because you're a citizen of a European Economic Area and you've lived in the 'common travel area' for the last 2 years you may be eligible to apply for benefits in the UK.

You need to bring the following items to a jobcentre to prove your right to live in the UK:

  • your passport or national ID card
  • proof of where you lived in the last 2 years
