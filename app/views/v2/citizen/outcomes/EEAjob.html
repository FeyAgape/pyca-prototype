You have the right to apply for benefits in the UK

Based on your answers, as a citizen of a European Economic Area country with a job in the UK you should have the right to apply for benefits in the UK.

You now need to bring the following items to a jobcentre to prove your right to live in the UK:

  • your passport or national ID card
  • proof of employment and how much you earn (eg recent wage slips or P60) or an offer of employment if you've not started working yet
  • proof of your partner's nationality
  • details of your partner's work, if relevant
  • your marriage certificate or proof of civil partnership, if relevant
