How to fast-track

  1. Go to the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do and in the ‘agent notes’ box, enter:
    • the claimant status (see the top of this page for claimant status)
    • the details of evidence you've collected
  2. Click ‘done’ to complete the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do.
  3. Remind this person to bring at least 3 months' bank account statement or cash book to their gateway interview.

Gateway interview to-do

You need to leave some PYCA instructions for the self-employment gateway interview work coach.

  1. Go to the ‘Gateway self-employment interview’ to-do
  2. In the ‘agent notes’ box, copy and paste the following:

    For PYCA (Prove You Can Apply) purposes, gateway work coach should do the following before filling the ‘decision notes’ box:

    • collect bank account statement or simple cash book covering at least 3 months
    • type here details of the item collected (for example, ‘I have seen the claimant’s bank statement from July-October 2016 and it shows earnings and regular business activity’)
    • email decision maker at stockport.pyca@dwp.gsi.gov.uk with the full name of the person claiming
  3. Save and close the ‘verify self-employment’ to-do
