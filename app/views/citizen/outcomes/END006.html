You need to book a residency test for this person

They may be able to apply for benefits because they’ve lived in the UK for more than 5 years. They need to do a residency test to prove they’ve been living in the UK for at least 5 years.

Book HRT appointment

Book a HRT appointment for them and explain what it is.

Put evidence list in their journal

Ask them to bring the following to their HRT appointment:

  • passport, residence card or BRP showing proof of permanent residency
  • proof of where you’ve lived in the last 5 years
  • details of your employment in the last 5years
  • proof of when you entered the UK

Then copy and paste the evidence list in the person's Universal Credit account journal. Let them know you have done this and that they can refer to it later.
