You're naturalised in the UK

Because you've lived in the UK for more than 5 years you should be able to apply for benefits.

You need to bring the following items to a jobcentre to prove your right to live in the UK:

  • your passport
  • proof of where you live, eg rental agreement

You will then have a short interview about whether you live and intend to live in the UK for the forseeable future. This will cover where you live and details of your daily life, for example your doctor or dentist.

If you have family in the UK

You'll be asked questions about your children, partner or any other family you have in the UK. This will include the dates they arrived, the school they go to and the work they do.

You don't have to give evidence of this, but you may find it handy to have these details to hand.

What to do next

Once you have your evidence call 0800 900 1900 to make an appointment at your local jobcentre.
