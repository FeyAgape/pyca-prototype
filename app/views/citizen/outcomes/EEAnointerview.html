You can apply for benefits in the UK

Based on your answers you're a single, employed worker from a country in the European Economic Area. This means you can apply for benefits as long as you can prove this.

You now need to bring the following items to a jobcentre:

  • your passport
  • some proof of your employment, eg wage slips or employment contract
  • some proof of where you live, eg a rent agreement or mortgage statement dated in the last 3 months

What to do next

Once you have your evidence call 0800 900 1900 to make an appointment at your local jobcentre.

Simply bring your evidence. It will be photocopied. Your appointment should take no more than 5 minutes.
