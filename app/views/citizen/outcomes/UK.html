You said you're a UK citizen

This usually means you can apply for benefits, however because you spent some time outside the UK in the last 2 years you have to prove you're currently living in the UK.

You now need to bring the following items to a jobcentre to prove you're living in the UK:

  • your passport
  • some proof of where you live, eg a rent agreement or mortgage statement dated in the last 3 months
  • details of any trips outside the UK that lasted more than 4 weeks

What to do next

Once you have your evidence call 0800 900 1900 to make an appointment at your local jobcentre.

You'll need to bring your evidence to be photocopied and answer a few questions.
