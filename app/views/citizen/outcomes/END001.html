You can apply for benefits in the UK

You said you were a UK citizen which usually means you can apply for benefits. However, when you set up your Universal Credit account you said you spent significant time outside the UK in the last 2 years. This means you need to prove you’re currently living in the UK.

What to do next

You now need to bring the following items to a jobcentre:

  • your passport or identity card
  • some proof of where you live, eg a rent agreement or mortgage statement dated in the last 3 months
  • details of any trips outside the UK that lasted more than 4 weeks
