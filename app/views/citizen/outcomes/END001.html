Don't do a HRT with this person

They're a UK citizen and can apply for benefits.

Evidence you need

  • current passport

What to do next

  1. Open the HRT entitlement action to-do
  2. In the agent notes box, type in:
    • details of the passport (date of issue, expiry date and passport number)
    • details of any trips outside the UK that lasted more than 4 weeks in the last 2 years (including the country they visited, reason for the visit and reason for their return)
  3. Download HRT entitlement action ALP and in the final box, type in ‘HRT trial’ (leave the other boxes blank)
  4. After you've saved and uploaded the pdf, close the HRT entitlement action to-do

    5. Don't create agent ‘refer to decision maker (HRT)’ to-do

  5. Email the decision maker at stockport.pyca@dwp.gsi.gov.uk and give the name of the person claiming
  6. Remind the person claiming to check their Universal Credit account later for decision
