How to fast-track

  1. In the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do, for the question ‘Is an HRT interview required?’, select ‘No’.
  2. Click ‘done’ to complete the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do.
  3. Create ‘refer to decision maker’ to-do.
  4. For the question ‘Is this a fast-track HRT referral case?’, select ‘Yes’.
  5. Complete the PYCA ALP
  6. Remind the person claiming to check their Universal Credit account for updates on their claim.
