Not sure if you’ve been given the right evidence?

Speak to your UC full service Single Point of Contact (SPOC).

How to fast-track

  1. Go to the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do and in the ‘agent notes’ box, enter:
    • the claimant status (see the top of this page for claimant status)
    • the details of each evidence you've collected
  2. Select .......... (PYCA or eHRT button)
  3. Click ‘done’ to complete the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do.
  4. Email the decision maker at stockport.pyca@dwp.gsi.gov.uk with the full name of the person claiming.
  5. Remind the person claiming to check their Universal Credit account for updates on their claim.
