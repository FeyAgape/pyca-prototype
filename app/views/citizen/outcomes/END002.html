Claimant can apply for benefits in the UK

The claimant is an employed worker from a country in the European Economic Area. This means they can apply for benefits as long as they can prove this.

Gather evidence documents

Ask the claimant to provide:

  • their passport, ID card, residence card or BRP
  • proof of accommodation
  • proof of employment that shows how much they earn, such as recent wage slips, P60 or employment contract
  • proof of marriage, civil partnership or relationship if they have a partner
