You can apply for benefits in the UK

Based on your answers you’re a single, employed worker from a country in the European Economic Area. This means you can apply for benefits as long as you can prove this.

What to do next

Go to the jobcentre on the date of your habitual residency interview and bring the following items with you:

  • your passport, identity card or biometric residence permit (BRP)
  • some proof of your employment that shows how much you earn, eg wage slips or employment contract
  • some proof of where you live, eg a rent agreement or mortgage statement dated in the last 3 months
