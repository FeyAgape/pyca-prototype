You can apply for benefits in the UK

Based on your answers you're an employed worker from a country in the European Economic Area. This means you can apply for benefits as long as you can prove this.

What to do next

Go to the jobcentre on the date of your next interview and bring the following items with you:

  • proof of your employment that shows how much you earn, such as recent wage slips, P60 or employment contract if you've not started working yet
