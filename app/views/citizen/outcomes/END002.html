Fast-track this {{ 'couple' if isPartnerFlow else 'person' }}

Claimant status

Employed EEA citizen with a qualifying partner

Evidence you need

  • current passport or residence card
  • date they first arrived in the UK
  • 3 months’ proof of employment that shows how much they earn (recent wage slips, employment contract or bank statement)

Not sure if you’ve been given the right evidence?

Call Petros on 01227 591204 (ext 02715) or Janice on 0161 9684612 (ext 78224) to check.

What to do next

  1. Open the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do and in the ‘agent notes’ box, enter the claimant status (see the top of this page for claimant status).
  2. Enter the details of each evidence you’ve collected:
    • passport – number, nationality, date of issue and expiry date
    • date they first arrived in the UK (exact date, otherwise month and year, or just year)
    • payslip – name of employer and earnings (including dates) or
    • employment contract – type and duration of contract, hours of work and earnings or
    • bank statement – earnings and period covered
  3. Click ‘done’ to complete the ‘prove you can apply’ to-do.
  4. Email the decision maker at stockport.pyca@dwp.gsi.gov.uk with the full name of the person claiming.
  5. Book a habitual residency test interview in the UCFS build (not Booking Bug) using any date and time.
  6. Go to the claimant’s to-do list, open ‘Attend your habitual residency interview’ to-do, select ‘attended’ and save.

  7. Open the partner’s ‘verify claimant identity’ to-do and in the agent notes box, write: ‘This person is the partner of a fast-tracked HRT claimant and they must also be fast-tracked.’
    8. {% endif -%}
  8. Remind the person claiming to check their Universal Credit account for a decision.
