Not sure if you’ve been given the right evidence?

Call Petros on 01227 591204 (ext 02715) or Janice on 0161 9684612 (ext 78224) to check.

Evidence you need

  • current passport or residence card
  • date they first arrived in the UK
  • 3 months’ proof of employment that shows how much they earn, such as recent wage slips, P60, employment contract or bank statement

What to do next

  1. Copy and paste the above evidence list into the ‘prove you can apply’ to do.
  2. Open the HRT entitlement action to do.
  3. In the agent notes box, enter the details of the evidence you’ve collected:
    • passport – number, date of issue and expiry date
    • date they first arrived in the UK (exact date, otherwise month and year, or just year)
    • payslip – name of employer and earnings (including dates)
    • employment contract – type and duration of contract, hours of work and earnings
    • bank statement – earnings and period covered
  4. Download HRT entitlement action ALP.
  5. Enter ‘HRT trial’ in the final box (leave other boxes blank).
  6. Save and upload the edited ALP.
    7. {%- if isPartnerFlow %}
  7. Open the partner’s HRT entitlement action to do.
  8. Add a note to the agent notes field stating: ‘Partner part of Stockport Trial, claimant must be referred to Stockport DM’.
    9. {% endif -%}
  9. Email the decision maker at stockport.pyca@dwp.gsi.gov.uk with the full name of the person claiming.
  10. Remind the person claiming to check their Universal Credit account for a decision.
  11. Book a ‘mock interview’ in the customer account and then immediately delete it so that it no longer appears as an outstanding task. You don’t need to book a slot in Booking Bug, so you can use any date.
