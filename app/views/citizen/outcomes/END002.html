Don't do a HRT with this person

They are an employed worker from a country in the European Economic Area. This means they can apply for benefits as long as they can prove this.

What to do next

1. Ask them for their:

  • passport, ID card, residence card or BRP
  • proof of where they live, such as a rent agreement or mortgage statement dated in the last 3 months
  • proof of employment that shows how much they earn, such as recent wage slips, P60, employment contract or bank statement
  • proof of marriage, civil partnership or relationship if they have a partner

2. Put the information in the HRT to-do

3. Send an email to the decision maker (stockport.pyca@dwp.gsi.gov.uk), giving details such as name of the person claiming

4. Remind the person claiming to check their Universal Credit account later for decision
