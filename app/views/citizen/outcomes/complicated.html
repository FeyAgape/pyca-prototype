You must speak to a work coach to find out if you can apply

It looks like you don't have the right to apply for benefits in the UK yourself but you may still be able to apply because you have family here.

You need to bring the following items to a jobcentre to prove your right to live in the UK:

  • your passport or identity card
  • proof of where you live, eg rental agreement, mortgage statement or utility bills

Your family information

You also need to bring proof of any of the following that applies to you:

  • your partner's nationality, eg their passport or identity card
  • your partner's work, eg their wage slips
  • your marriage or civil partnership
  • details of any other family you have living in the UK, including information about where they work and when they entered the UK
  • the school your child goes to
