You may be able to apply for benefits in the UK

You don't have the right to apply for benefits in the UK yourself, but you may be able to through your partner.

You need to bring the following items to a jobcentre to prove your right to live in the UK:

  • your passport
  • proof of employment (wage slip or similar)
  • your partner's passport
  • proof of your partner's employment (wage slip or similar)
  • proof of your marriage or civil partnership
  • proof of dates both of you arrived in the country, if not stamped in your passport
