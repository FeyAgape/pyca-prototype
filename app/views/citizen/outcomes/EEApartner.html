Your partner is an EEA citizen

You don't have the right to apply for benefits in the UK yourself, but you may be able to through your partner.

You need to bring the following items to a jobcentre to prove your right to live in the UK:

  • your passport
  • proof of employment (wage slip or similar)
  • your partner's passport
  • proof of your partner's employment (wage slip or similar)
  • proof of your marriage or civil partnership

If you have other family in the UK

You'll be asked questions about your children or any other family you have in the UK. This will include the dates they arrived, the school they go to and the work they do.

You don't have to give evidence of this, but you may find it handy to have these details to hand.

What to do next

Once you have your evidence call 0800 900 1900 to make an appointment at your local jobcentre.
