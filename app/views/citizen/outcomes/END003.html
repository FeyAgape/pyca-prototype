You need to book a residency test for this person

They don't have the right to apply for benefits in the UK, but they may be able to through their partner.

Book HRT appointment

Book a HRT appointment for them and explain what it is.

Put evidence list in their journal

Ask them to bring the following to their HRT appointment:

  • passport, residence card or BRP
  • proof of accommodation
  • proof of employment if you work, such as recent wage slips, P60 or employment contract
  • proof of your marriage, civil partnership or relationship
  • proof of date you arrived in the country

Then copy and paste the evidence list in their Universal Credit account journal. Let them know you have done this and that they can refer to it later.
