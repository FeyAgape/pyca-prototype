You should have the right to apply for benefits in the UK

As a citizen of a European Economic Area country with a job in the UK you should be able to apply for benefits in the UK.

You now need to bring the following items to a jobcentre to prove your right to live in the UK:

  • your passport or national ID card
  • proof of your self-employment, for example your accounts and company registration if relevant
