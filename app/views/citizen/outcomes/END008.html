Don't do a HRT with this person

They have the right to apply for benefits in the UK as long as they can prove they're a refugee.

Evidence you need

  • BRP (biometric residence permit), passport or Home Office document that says they're a refugee

What to do next

  1. Open the HRT entitlement action to-do
  2. In the agent notes box, type in details of the evidence you've collected such as:
    • passport number, date of issue, expiry date and visa status
    • BRP card issue date, unique permit number on the right corner of the card, type of permit and any other details at the back of the card (such as 'EU right to reside')
    • date of the Home Office document and any refugee status details
  3. Download HRT entitlement action ALP and in the final box, type in ‘HRT trial’ (leave the other boxes blank)
  4. After you've saved and uploaded the pdf, close the HRT entitlement action to-do

    5. Don't create agent ‘refer to decision maker (HRT)’ to-do

  5. Email the decision maker at stockport.pyca@dwp.gsi.gov.uk and give the full name of the person claiming
  6. Remind the person claiming to check their Universal Credit account later for a decision
