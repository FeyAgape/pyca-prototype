Don't do a residency test with this person

They have the right to apply for benefits in the UK as long as they can prove they're a refugee.

What to do next

1. Ask the person for their BRP or any official UK Home Office document that says they’re a refugee

2. In the HRT to-do, record details from the BRP or document, including date issued, unique permit number from the right hand corner on the front of the card and the type of permit from the front of the card (see how to check these documents)

3. Send an email to the decision maker (stockport.pyca@dwp.gsi.gov.uk) and give name of the person claiming

4. Remind the person claiming to check their Universal Credit account later for decision
