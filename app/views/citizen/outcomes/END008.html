You should be able to apply for benefits in the UK

You have the right to apply for benefits in the UK as long as you remain a refugee.

What to do next

Go to the jobcentre on the date of your habitual residency interview and bring the following items with you:

  • passport or other proof of your identity
  • your letter from the UK Home Office that says you’re a refugee
