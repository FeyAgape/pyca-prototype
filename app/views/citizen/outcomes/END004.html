Claimant may be able to apply for benefits in the UK

The claimant is from a country in the European Economic Area and they’ve lived in the 'common travel area' for the last 2 years. As long as they can prove this, they may qualify for benefits in the UK.

Book HRT appointment

Book a HRT appointment for the claimant and explain what it is.

Put evidence list in claimant’s journal

Ask the claimant to bring the following evidence to their HRT appointment:

  • passport, ID card, residence card or BRP
  • proof of where you lived in the last 2 years

Then copy and paste the evidence list in the claimant’s Universal Credit account journal. Let them know you have done this and that they can refer to it later.
