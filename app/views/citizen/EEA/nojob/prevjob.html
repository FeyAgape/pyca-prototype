You're an EEA citizen

You need to bring the following items to a jobcentre to prove your right to live in the UK:

  • your passport or identity card
  • proof of your most recent employment in the UK (wageslips, P45 or P60)

You'll also need to have a short interview about whether you live, and intend to live, in the UK.

What to do next

Once you have your evidence call 0800 900 1900 to make an appointment at your local jobcentre.
