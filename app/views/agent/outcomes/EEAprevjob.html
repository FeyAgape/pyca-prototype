You need to do a residency interview

Because they're a national of a country in the European Economic Area but not currently working in the UK they may or may not qualify to apply for benefits in the UK.

What to do next

1. Scan the following items:

  • the person's passport or id card
  • the proof of where they live
  • the proof of any former employment they have

2. Carry out the residency interview using the tool as normal.
