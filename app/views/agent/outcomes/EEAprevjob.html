You need to do a residency test with this person

Because they're a national of a country in the European Economic Area but not currently working in the UK they may or may not qualify to apply for benefits in the UK. A decision maker will have to look at their case.

What to do next

1. Scan the following items:

  • the person's passport or id card
  • the proof of where they live
  • the proof of any former employment they have

2. Carry out the residency interview using the tool as normal.
