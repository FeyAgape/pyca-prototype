You don't need to do a residency test with this person

Because they've got a visa that proves their right to apply this person will qualify to apply for benefits regardless of anything in the habitual residency test.

As long as you've seen the visa you don't need to carry out an interview.

What to do next

1. Scan the following items:

  • the person's passport or photo ID
  • the visa, clearly showing the section where it says the person has 'recourse to public funds'

2. Tell the person they can leave and to check their Universal Credit account for the result of their residency check

3. Send the scanned documents to the specialist decision maker in the Stockport hub - hrtAlpha@dwp.gsi.gov.uk
