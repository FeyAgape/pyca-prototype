You need to do a residency test for this person

They don’t have the right to apply for benefits in the UK themselves, but they may be able to through their partner.

What to do next

1. Scan the following items:

  • the person’s passport or id card
  • the proof of where they live
  • proof of their employment (wage slip or similar)
  • their partner’s passport or photo id
  • proof of their partner’s employment (wage slip or similar)

2. Carry out a residency interview using the residency tool as normal.
