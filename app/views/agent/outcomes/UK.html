You don't need to do a residency test with this person

As long as you've seen evidence that the person is a UK citizen you don't need to carry out a full residency test.

What to do next

1. Scan the following items:

  • the person's passport or id card
  • the proof of where they live

2. Tell the person they can leave and to check their Universal Credit account for the result of their residency check

3. Send the scanned documents to the specialist decision maker in the Stockport hub - hrtAlpha@dwp.gsi.gov.uk
