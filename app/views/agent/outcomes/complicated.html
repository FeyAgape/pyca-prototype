You need to do a residency interview with this person

It looks like they don't have the right to apply for benefits themselves but they may still be able to apply because they have family here.

What to do next

1. Scan any of the following items they've brought with them:

  • their passport or identity card
  • proof of their most recent employment in the UK (wageslips, P45 or P60)
  • proof of when they first entered the UK
  • proof of their partner's nationality, eg passport or identity card
  • proof of their partner's work, eg wage slips
  • their marriage certificate or proof of civil partnership, if relevant

If their partner isn't from the EEA, you should also scan the following:

  • passport or photo ID and proof of address of any other family they have in the UK
  • details of the school their child goes to, if relevant

2. Carry out a residency interview using the residency tool as normal.
