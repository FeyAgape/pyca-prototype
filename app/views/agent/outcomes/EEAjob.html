You don't need to do a residency test for this person

Because they're a national of a country in the European Economic Area and are working in the UK they qualify to apply for benefits in the UK regardless of anything in the habitual residency test.

As long as you've seen evidence that the person is a citizen of an EEA country and is working in the UK you don't need to carry out a full interview.

What to do next

1. Scan the following items:

  • the person's passport or id card
  • the proof of where they live

2. Tell the person they can leave and to check their Universal Credit account for the result of their residency check.

3. Send the scanned documents to the specialist decision maker in the Stockport hub - hrtAlpha@dwp.gsi.gov.uk
