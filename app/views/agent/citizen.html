Ask the person: "Are you a UK citizen?"

They're a UK citizen if they have a British or Channel Islands passport, or live in the UK and have applied for and been given nationality since arriving.

You should ask them for their passport or photo ID and check their nationality. If not British then change your answer to 'no'.
